Automation Hub

What is the Automation Hub?

The Automation Hub runs background rules that monitor your pipeline and automatically take action — so your team never misses a follow-up or forgets a key step.

⚡
Rules — Conditions that trigger actions automatically. Example: "If no activity on a deal for 7 days → send a stale-deal alert."
🔔
Notifications — Alerts generated by rules. They appear as badges on the bell icon and here in the log.
🔗
Webhooks — Optional. Send data to external tools (Zapier, Slack, etc.) when rules fire.
📜
Automation Log — A history of every rule that fired, when, and for which contact.

Rules run automatically when the app loads and when data changes. You can create custom rules with the "+ New Rule" button above.

What does the Score number mean?

Each contact gets a CRM Health Score (0–100) that combines three factors to show how strong and active each relationship is:

📅
Recency (R) — How recently you last contacted them. Recent = higher score. No contact in 60+ days tanks it.
🔄
Frequency (F) — How often you interact (calls, emails, meetings in last 90 days). More touchpoints = higher score.
💰
Monetary (M) — The dollar value of deals associated with this contact. Higher pipeline value = higher score.

Combined into a single number: 🔴 Hot (70+) = strong, active relationship. 🟡 Warm (40–69) = moderate engagement. 🔵 Cold (0–39) = needs attention. Clients automatically get a boost.

⚡ Auto-populated from: overdue follow-up dates · hot lead scores · stale deals · contacts with no touch in 14+ days
